How to play Maze Runner 1
Objective
To collect all of the heart points and get to the skull face
with minimal interference with the enemies.
Move around the maze locating yourself strategicaly away from the enemies
Hunter starts with a lifepoint of 100 and it gets reduced every time he gets shot
When his life points reach 0. The game ends,
regardless of how many life points he has collected
StoryLine
Hunter is the last human in the city of Rode
He wants to make his way to Mars, the New Home
In order for him to do that he has to collect enought heart entities
and then return to Dr Swade so that he can build the spaceship
Killers
The killers are aliens from the unknown world
We know nothing of them, we do not know who they are
where they are from or what they really can do.
